REDONGRA Exchange Program

The REDONGRA Exchange Program supports and funds visits by young researchers (PhD students and postdocs) to different nodes of the network. This program aims to:

  • Foster collaboration between network members
  • Facilitate knowledge transfer and skill development
  • Encourage interdisciplinary research in gravitational wave physics
  • Strengthen the Spanish gravitational wave community

Program Details

Funding

The program provides financial support for travel and accommodation expenses, with a maximum of €1,500 per visit.

Duration

Visits typically last between 1 week and 1 month, with flexibility depending on the research needs.

Eligibility

Open to PhD students and postdocs (within 5 years of PhD completion) affiliated with REDONGRA institutions.

Application Process

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. Applicants should submit the form below at least 6 weeks before the proposed visit start date. Decisions are typically made within 2 weeks of submission.
